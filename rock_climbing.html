Outdoor Rock Climbing Experience

Challenge yourself on Hawaii's stunning natural rock formations

Experience the thrill of outdoor rock climbing in some of Hawaii's most spectacular settings. Our certified guides will lead you up natural rock faces while ensuring your safety and helping you build confidence on the rock.

Whether you're a complete beginner or have some climbing experience, we'll tailor the experience to your skill level. All technical equipment is provided, and our patient instruction will have you scaling new heights in no time.

Beyond the physical challenge, rock climbing offers a unique perspective on Hawaii's geological wonders and stunning landscapes that few visitors get to experience.

Before You Climb

Important information for your safety and enjoyment

What to Bring

  • Comfortable athletic clothing you can move in
  • Closed-toe shoes with good grip (sneakers work fine)
  • Water bottle and snacks
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Camera for those epic summit shots!

Physical Requirements

  • Moderate fitness level recommended
  • No prior climbing experience required
  • Ages 12+ (minors must be accompanied by guardian)
  • Weight limit: 250 lbs for safety equipment

Safety & Weather

  • Tours may be rescheduled due to weather conditions
  • All safety equipment provided and inspected
  • Certified guides with wilderness first aid training
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention

Cancellation Policy

  • Full refund with 48+ hours notice
  • 50% refund with 24-48 hours notice
  • Weather cancellations receive full refund
  • Rescheduling available based on availability
